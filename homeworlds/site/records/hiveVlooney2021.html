The Hive vs Andrew Looney
April 3, 2021
This game was played over Zoom as part of the Looney Labs Silver Jubilee celebration.
The Hive team consisted of

  • Steve Swarmer (captain)
    • Steve had the final say on what moves the team made. This was the only formal role with an official duty. The captain assignment was made by Laurie, and Steve made the informal assignments below.
  • Jonathan Baker (science officer)
  • Andrew Shirkey and Ryan Stapleton (helmsmen)
  • Laurie Menke (communications officer)
    • Laurie mainly attended as meeting organizer, but she gave some strategy suggestions as well.

The Hive won the coin toss for first move.
