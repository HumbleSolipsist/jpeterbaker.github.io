If West does not catastrophe the reds in East's home, East will win on their turn by moving g3 to West's home and calling catastrophe. The red catastrophe can only be achieved with the yellow sacrifice that starts turn 1 above.

Now, West must defend their home from direct assault, but there is no time to get a large ship to their home and simultaneously obtain a red ship before East's large red arrives.

East should green-teleport the g2

After 1., All of East's moves can be considered individually.

East could settle for a draw by moving (or green-teleporting) g3 to West's home for a catastrophe. But East would prefer to make the game a little longer and win.

Green-teleporting the g1 to sytem 2 is a promising move, but as will be seen, green-teleporting the g2 is much stronger.

Trading g3 for some other color allows West to build the g3, leaving East without any chance of threatening a green catastrophe.
