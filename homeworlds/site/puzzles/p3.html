In a previous version of this page, I posted poor solution: "move y2 Altair Alpha." Alpha has a reasonably good defense against this with "sacrifice y2 Alpha; move y3 Betelgeuse Alpha; move y1 Alpha Castor." Beta may still be able to win from here, but it's likely to be messy.

Beta wins quickly after

It's a great exercise to look at all of Alpha's possible responses and how the game would play out. I'm not going to lay it all out here at the moment (maybe if I get time later).

This position is from a ladder game on SDG where Beta was challenging Alpha for position #1. At this point in the game, Beta played

1... discover r1 Beta y3 Deneb

and probably intended to continue with

2... sacrifice y2 Altair
move r2 Altair Alpha
move r2 Altair Alpha
3... move r1 Deneb Alpha
catastrophe Alpha r

but Alpha responded with

2. sacrifice y2 Betelgeuse
move r1 Betelgeuse Altair
move r2 Castor Altair
catastrophe Altair r
