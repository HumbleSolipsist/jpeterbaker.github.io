Homeworlds Rules: Quick Reference

My main Homeworlds page

On your turn

First turn: choose two stars and a ship for your homeworld.

On all other turns, choose one:

  1. Basic action: Chose one of your ships and empower it with the technology color of a star or any one of your ships in that system.
  2. Sacrifice: Choose one of your ships and return it to the bank. Then choose one or more of your ships and empower them with the technology of the sacrificed ship. The number of other ships you can empower is the number of pips on the sacrificed ship.
  3. Pass: Take no action.

Catastrophes

If four pieces of the same color are in the same system, you may immediately return them to the bank.

Technologies

  • Red: capture. Take control of an enemy ship in the same system that is the same size or smaller than your empowered ship.
  • Yellow: move. Move the empowered ship to a connected system (one with no star sizes that match the ship's current system). Alternatively, take any connected star from the bank and have your ship discover it as a new system.
  • Green: build. Take the smallest bank piece in the empowered ship's color and add it as a ship to the empowered ship's system.
  • Blue: trade. Exchange the empowered ship with any piece of the same size from the bank.

Winning

You win if your opponent controls no ships in their homeworld.
