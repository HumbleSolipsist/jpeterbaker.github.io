Looney vs Cooper

Andrew Looney vs John Cooper
Looneycon exhibition team game (July 22, 2016)
Six of the first attendees to volunteer were put up as potential teammates.
Cooper won the coin toss for first pick, so Looney got the first move in the match.
Cooper selected Joshua Kronengold and Joshua Denmeade as teammates.
Looney's teammates were Dayle Hodge and Leila Zucker.
Ordinary sudden death time control was used. Each team began with 25 minutes on the game clock.
