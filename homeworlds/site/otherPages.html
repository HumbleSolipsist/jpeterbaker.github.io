Playing Online

  • Homeworlds Live is the first site dedicated to real-time Homeworlds play. The site launched in September 2020, and at the time I'm writing this, there aren't enough users that you are likely to find anyone online unless you arrange it beforehand. Because of the tool the site is built on (called "Glitch"), the site takes a few minutes to start up when you first request it, but it runs normally after that.
