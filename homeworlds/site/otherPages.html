Playing Online

  • Board Game Arena has my own Homeworlds implementation, which has proved to be fairly popular.
  • Homeworlds Live was the first site dedicated to real-time Homeworlds play. The site launched in September 2020, and at the time I'm writing this, there aren't enough users that you are likely to find anyone online unless you arrange it beforehand.
  • Super Duper Games (SDG) lets you play many abstract games by correspondence, Homeworlds being the most popular.
  • Anywhere Board Games (ABG) is software you can use to play any board game, sort of like a simpler 2D Tabletop Simulator.
    • More about ABG here on my site (including ABG files for Homeworlds )
  • Tabletop Simulator (TTS) with the Homeworlds module.
