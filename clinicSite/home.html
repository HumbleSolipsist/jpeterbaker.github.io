Services

The Valley Veterinary Clinic offers exams, vaccinations, and lab work as well as medical, dental and surgical procedures for your pet.

Driving Directions

When you are by Huntsville at the intersection of 7800 East and 100 South, turn East on 100 South (Hwy. 39) and go 2.75 miles. We are on the north side of the road at 10010 East.

History

Dr. Etta Baker graduated in 1977 from Washington State University with a Doctor's in Veterinary Medicine. After becoming a partner in the Rollin's and Stock Veterinary Hospital in Roy Utah she opened the Clearfield Veterinary Clinic in Clearfield Utah. Leaving there in 1987 Dr. Baker opened the Valley Veterinary Hospital in Huntsville, on the farm where she grew up.
